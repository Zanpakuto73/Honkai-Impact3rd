Costumes

Costumes have no effect on gameplay. They just given a game character more personality or make them more unique. Even if the costumes give no bonus, they are really cool.

Gardenia

Cromson Impulse has been dyed with the hues of Gardenia, the symbol of "eternal love and promise'. [Equip on: Crimson Impulse]

Azure

This Azure armor represents the calm, deep ocean. [Equip on: Crimson Impulse]

Midnight Marigold

At night, a withered sakura bloom caresses the tip of the candle's flames, its translucent petals revealing a golden, kaleidoscopic color. At least this is what the costume designer said. [Equip on: Gyakushinn Miko]

Ocean Ranger

Kiana invincible on land! So why not in the ocean, too! [Equip on: Valkyrie Ranger]

Aeterna Purum

Amidst the sounds of laughter and celebration, a young, elegantly dressed girl emerged with bouquet in hand said with an expression of hope,"Ne~, keep this day in your memories." [Equip on: Valkyrie Bladestrike]

Origin

This is the Prototype Fusion-class Battlesuit designed to accommodate massive amounts of power. Not many of these suits are around nowadays. [Equip on: Scarlet Fusion]

Vast Ocean

The Valkyries love lazing in the sun on a tropical beach while wearing these swimsuits. They can definitely use a playful dip as well. This summer couldn't get any hotter than this! [Equip on: Crimson Impulse, Shadow Dash, Valkyrie Bladestrike, Lightning Empress]
