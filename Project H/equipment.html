Stigmata

Stigmata was technology left behind a civiliaztion destroyed by Honkai 50,000 years ago. They are genes for fightinga against Honkai. In order to fight back Honkai, a group analyzed the stigmata and created technology for transplanting arificial stigmata. However, as a side effect, it will decrease one's own lifespan.
