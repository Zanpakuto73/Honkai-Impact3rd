Valkyria

Valkryja are disposable soldiers with stigmata implanted in them and have the ability to transform collapsed energy into battle. Most Valkyrja are forced to use man made stigmata because they do not have a natural resistance to Honkai energy at birth. In the game, there are a few valkryia that are playable. Below are just a few.

Kiana Kaslana

One of the first Valkryjia to play as in the game. She is born between two powerful familes: Kaslana and Shania. The Kaslana have excellent ability to control Honkai energy and the Kaslana are born with good Honkai energy adaptibility. She attends St.Freya's Academy for other valkryia.Principal Theresa,the head of the school, is her relative. Kiana attends school with her friend and love interest Mei and Bronya. Her main choice of weapons is dual guns. Her variations are but not limited to:

  • White Comet- High survivability. Melee attacker with constant damage per second(DPS).
  • Valkryie Ranger- Uses ranged gun attacks to provide support.
  • Divine Prayer- Melee attack with high damage per second(DPS). Provides large health recovery for others.

Raiden Mei

Mei was the daughter of the ME(Master Electric) Company CEO. At age 8, a natrual stigmata was found on her body and her father sealed it. At one point, the company went through a financial crisis. She, along with her father, was arrested. Her personality changed because of this event but it soon returned after metting Kiana and her friends. During an incidentin Chouluu City, they have found that Mei contains the 3rd Herrscher, a massive amount of energy forms inside a person's body with its own personality. It is similar to being a spokesperson of Honkai. Both Kiana and Bronya look up to her as an elder sister. In combat, she wields a katana. The Raiden family traditional martial art technique was passed to down to Mei. She uses a new a combination of swordsmanship and ninja art. Mei's new combination created a new mysterious dighting style. With ninja arts, she can frelly perform shukuchi (rapid dashing move) and bunshin(clone technique). Her variations are but not limited to:

  • Crimson Impulse- Melee attacker with slash attack. Stable area of effect(AOE) damage within small radius.
  • Shadow Dash- Middle-ranged attacker with high DPS and she can summon clones.
  • Lightning Empress- Melee attacker with slash attack. Possess pure elemental power of lightning.

Bronya Zaychik

At a young age, Bronya was orphaned, picked up by army, and raised into a legendary assassin. One day, one of her targets captured Bronya and put her into an orphanage. There, Bronya befriends Seele. There kids were raised to be test subjects for an experiment, Bronya, unbeknown to her, was replaced by Seele because of a leg injury. However, Seele's body disappeared. Desperate to reunite with her, Bronya does the experiment as well and catches a glimpse of her only to have Seele convince her to keep living. Bronya listens to her, but she is inflicted wtih brain damage from doing the experiment too long From there, she would act as a spy inside St.Freya Academy, where she befriends Kiana and Mei. Bronya has a special deviceattached to her called Bunny 19C which allows her to use an array of heavy weapons. Her variations are but not limited to:

  • Valkyrie Chariot- Excels at ranged DPS and possesses powerful support abilities.
  • Black Nucleus- Close-ranged burst attack. Deals deadly blow from enemy's back after it becomes indivisble.
  • Yamabuki Armor- Ranged attacker with high DPS. Can protect whole team with a Mecha shield.

Theresa Apocalypse

Theresa is the the founder and current Headmaster of St. Fraya Academy. She is the grand daughter of Otto Apocalypse. However, she is not related by his blood. She is a clone of a Valkyrie Kallen Kaslana and a Honkai beast named Vishnu. Because of the honkai cells, she is able to regenerate her limbs immediately if one is lost. Despite her being immortal, her body growth has stopped. Now, her physical age only reaces 12 years old. She at one point was friends with Kiana's mother and became her godparent. Even though she is tough on Kiana, she is very protective of her. Theresa main weapon of choice is a cross. Her variations are but not limited to:

  • Valkyrie Pledge- Middle Ranged AOE suppressing. Weapon skills boost damage.
  • Sakuno Rondo- Middle-ranged flexible DPS. Deals massive fire damage.
