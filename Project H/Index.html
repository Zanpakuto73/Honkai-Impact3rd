What is Honkai Impact 3rd?

For people that wandered onto this site by accident, Honkai Impact 3rd
is a game developed by Mihoyo. It is played on platforms such as tablets
and phones. The game is free to play. It is an anime role playing game
where female characters are engaged in constant battle. They use either
swords, guns, or energy weapns to fight off hordes of enemies.The
gameplay is shown to be similar to that of Bayonetta for an example.
