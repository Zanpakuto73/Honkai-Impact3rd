Stigmata

Later in the game, the enemies get tougher, and the fights start to last longer with the increase of difficulty. To boost the Valkyria, one uses stigmata. Stigmata are genes used to fight against Honkai beasts. Early in the game, common stigmata will appear, but in the later stages, it is essential to get more powerful ones to deal more damage. Each stigmata can increase health, attack, defense, and critical. There are 3 slots to equip stigmata on Valkyria. The top(T), middle(M), and bottom(B). When there is 2 or 3 of the same kind of stigmata in the slots, more benefits will appear.
